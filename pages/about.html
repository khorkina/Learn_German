About Learn German Daily

Our Mission

Learn German Daily is a free, privacy-focused platform for learning German from beginner (A1) to advanced (C1) levels. We believe language learning should be accessible, structured, and respectful of your privacy.

How It Works

All lessons are structured around the CEFR levels (A1-C1) and provide daily practice with grammar explanations, vocabulary, and interactive exercises. Your progress is tracked automatically as you complete lessons and exercises.

Privacy & Data Storage

Your learning data is stored entirely in your browser using IndexedDB (or localStorage as a fallback). This means:

  • No account required
  • No data sent to any server
  • Your progress stays on your device
  • You're in complete control of your data

If you clear your browser data, your progress will be lost. We recommend using the same device and browser for consistency.

Content Approach

All lessons and exercises are original content inspired by established German learning curricula but created independently. We do not reproduce copyrighted textbook materials.
