Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

Data Storage

Learn German Daily stores all your learning data locally in your web browser using IndexedDB or localStorage. This includes:

  • Lesson progress and completion status
  • Vocabulary you've learned
  • Exercise results and scores
  • Learning streaks and statistics
  • Personal preferences and settings

No Server Storage

We do not store any of your data on our servers. All data remains on your device. This means:

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No data transmission to servers
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • Your progress is private and local to your browser

Data Portability

Since your data is stored locally, you can:

  • Clear it anytime by clearing your browser data
  • Access it only from the browser where you learned

Important: If you clear your browser's cache/data or use a different browser or device, you will lose your progress.

Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking. The site only uses essential local storage for saving your progress.

Advertising (Optional)

This site may display ads through Google AdSense. If enabled:

  • Google may collect data for ad personalization
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your Google Ad Settings
  • Ads are placed unobtrusively to respect your learning experience

Third-Party Services

If we use third-party services (like AdSense), they may have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing:

  • Google Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
